What Are PCB Defects?

PCBs (Printed Circuit Boards) are essential in all electronic devices. Defects such as broken traces, pad misalignments, short circuits, or solder bridges can severely affect device performance, reliability, and safety. These defects lead to increased manufacturing costs, delays, and quality issues during mass production.

About This ML Project

This project uses Machine Learning to predict whether a PCB is faulty or OK based on various process parameters and inspection results like acid concentration, temperature, electrical test values, and visual inspections. The model is trained using real or simulated manufacturing data and applies both algorithmic prediction and logical rules to increase reliability. It can help in early fault detection, saving time and reducing waste.
